Types

In fish stores, sometimes crayfish are divided by colors, the most common being orange, white and blue. However, there’s a problem in this way of categorization: the orange and the white are different varieties of the same species Procambarus clarki, while the blue are of another species Procambarus alleni. Problems like this occur very often to the naming of all crayfish, so I prefer introducing them separately by their biological taxonomy.

分類

在水族館裡，有時螯蝦的種類是用顏色來區分，而最常見的就是橘、白、藍的「三色螯蝦」。然而，這種分類法有個問題：橘螯蝦和白螯蝦屬於同一個品種Procambarus clarki（克氏原螯蝦、美國螯蝦），而藍螯蝦則屬於另一個品種Procambarus alleni（佛羅里達藍螯蝦）。像這樣的命名問題在各種螯蝦身上都時有所聞，因此我個人偏好依據生物分類學的方式分別介紹牠們。
