What are crayfish

Crayfish are a type of cute crustaceans that has two pinchers. Most of the pet species are sturdy and could tolerate a wide range of water parameter, so they don’t die as easily as betta fish or goldfish. They come in different shapes, colors and sizes, and although the actual required tank size would depend on the size of the species, most of them don’t need a big tank. In fact, because they are very space-efficient pets, many keepers ended up collecting many of them(I personally keep 3 in my 10 square meter room). They will generally be fine if you forget to feed them for an entire week, and when you do feed them they basically eat everything. In short, they are perfect pets for people living in the urban area. Here on this website I will briefly introduce the characteristics of three species of crayfish that I currently own.

螯蝦是什麼

螯蝦是種可愛的甲殼類，有兩隻大螯因而得名。大部分做為寵物的品種都生命力都非常強韌，對各式各樣的水質都很能適應，所以牠們不像鬥魚或金魚一樣容易死去。牠們的體型、顏色和大小都非常多樣化。雖然每隻螯蝦實際需要的魚缸大小取決於螯蝦本身的尺寸，但大部分螯蝦都不需要很大的魚缸就能飼養；因為養螯蝦不太需要很大的空間，許多飼主最終都會收集非常多不同的種類（我自己就在我三坪的房間裡養了三隻）。一般來說，就算你整個禮拜都忘記餵食，牠們也能活得好好的；餵食上也沒有什麼要注意的地方，因為牠們什麼都吃。簡而言之，牠們對都市人來說是完美的寵物。在這個網站裡，我會簡單介紹我目前飼養的三種螯蝦。
